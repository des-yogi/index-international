Indicators

Is a tool for policymakers to compare national policy frameworks for sustainable energy and identify opportunities to attract investment. Assesses countries’ policy support for each of the three pillars of sustainable energy – access to electricity, access to clean cooking (for 55 access-deficit countries), energy efficiency, and renewable energy. With over 30 indicators covering 138 countries and representing over 98 percent of the world population, provides a reference point to help policymakers benchmark their sector policy and regulatory framework against those of regional and global peers, and a powerful tool to help develop policies and regulations that advance sustainable energy goals.
